Aspiring Software Developer

I'm an Actuarial Science graduate from Sunway University, but honestly? Tech has always been where my curiosity lives.

Like many, I graduated right as COVID hit — so I pivoted into a Data Analyst internship, got my hands dirty with data cleaning, prep, and reporting, and never looked back.

I took that foundation to NielsenIQ, where I joined Operations as a Data Processing Specialist supporting the New Zealand Reference Data Team. On top of that, I stepped up as the team's Quality Representative — which meant owning issue resolution and making sure our data stayed accurate and reliable.

But I wanted to build things, not just analyze them. That itch for automation brought me to 2X Marketing, through a friend's recommendation. There, I hit the ground running — building automations, writing scripts, managing email marketing flows, running system audits, and juggling a growing stack of tools. It was chaotic in the best way.

Right now, I'm diving headfirst into Software Development — not just coding, but really understanding how systems tick. I'm actively building this very site as my portfolio/CV, and I'm working through a Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp alongside learning CMS platforms like Webflow. I'm looking for roles where I can blend my data logic with my growing dev skills — and honestly, I'm excited to see where that takes me.
